“What excites me about design?”

It was not a long time ago I discovered my interest in designing products and user experiences for people to meet their goals. In 2018 while chatting with a friend, who was working on his start-up, we started to discuss the product and its flows which I found quite engrossing and that’s when I discovered my interest in creating engaging and delightful experiences. I started to read more about it, took a few online courses and established my passion. That’s when I decided to go back to school and learn about design principles and how to build helpful products.

Generally, I enjoy connecting the dots, questioning things and figuring out how it all works together. I like solving problems and simplifying the complex. Even at work I loved to analyze the problems - talked to the teams, understood the actual problem, got to the root cause and provided solutions. I always ensured I understood the problem right and found better ways to solve them. Also, I have the ability to understand people from their own perspective.

Having worked as a Software Developer for all these years I thought it is time to harness my creative energy.
