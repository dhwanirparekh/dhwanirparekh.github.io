“What excites me about design?”

I stumbled across design during my first internship as a UI/UX Designer at TDLab. Working alongside other passionate developers, designers, and product managers, I developed a toolbox in user-interface design, rapid prototyping, and usability testing. Later on at CIBC, I discovered my interest in user experience research and the importance of uncovering insights from analytics to drive meaningful design decisions.

Through all this, I’ve realized that my passion for design stems from a desire to make a lasting and profound positive impact. As a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo, I am taught not only how to build a product for the user, but also how to analyze its intersections with society and the environment. As such, I constantly find myself thinking about ways in which we can create a more accessible, inclusive, and sustainable place to live.

It's a complicated world out there, and I'm ready to tackle new problems that come my way, one case study at a a time.
